Corona Engineering Ltd. (CEL) is one of the leading electrical engineering companies in Bangladesh representing several global partners with whom we strive to bring the highest quality product home. We provide complete solutions in the following fields:
  • Electrical and Automation Processes
  • Control Products
  • Low-Voltage, Medium Voltage and High Voltage Switchgear
  • Electrical Components such as Surge Protectors and Isolators
  • Transformers - Both dry and oil type of all capacities
  • Power Factor Corrector (PFI) Panels
  • Complete Bus-Bar Solutions
  • Automatic Voltage Regulator
  • Motor Control Centre Panels
  • Industrial UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) Solutions
  • Complete BMS and SCADA Solutions

These products are of the highest quality, having been tested in world-renowned European Institutions and hold esteemed certificates such as ISO, ASTA and KEMA. Here at Corona we strive to ensure that you never have to settle for anything less than the very best.

Our product and solutions portfolio is further complemented by a wide range of after sales services in engineering, construction and maintenance. You will always have access to the very best support, capable of being dispatched at a moments notice to fix any problems you may have. We take considerable pride in the fact that any customer of ours will readily verify and backup this claim.

  • Office Complexes

  • Industrial Complexes in Textiles, Ceramics, Glass Processing Plants, Chemicals, Cement Plant, Petrochemicals, Gas Processing Plants, Steel Plants, Hospitals, Pharmaceuticals, Hotels, Power Plants and more are added with each passing day.

CPGL is one of the leading Power Generating Equipment suppliers in Bangladesh and provide complete solution for standby and prime power generation to our clients. Corona Power Generation Ltd (CPGL) has supplied many Diesel Generating sets equipped with Perkins and Cummins Engine. CPGL is also working as EPC.

Whether you are looking for generators, automatic transfer switches, paralleling switchgear, generators for a complete integrated power generation system, finding the best power solution takes a relationship with someone who understands your challenges the way we do.

CPGL is delivering everything from complete project design and management to long term plant operation, generator maintenance and service.

CPGL matches the right generator set and transfer and control technologies with your power needs. Standby emergency power, distributed power generation, prime power, co-generation, waste to energy. Your power related requirements may be varied and we have efficient solutions designed to meet demands. With our pre-integrated onsite power system, all the power system components are designed to work together as a whole.
